Let us talk about
Google One.

Google One is the new subscription-based storage plan from Google and should not be compared to Google Drive. Basically, Google provides you with basic 15GB of storage that is distributed between your Gmail account, Google Photos, Drive and other services that company provides but if you want to access more storage then either you have to compress your files that might lose the originality of it or you have to delete the files, which is stupid. You can also access paid options which sound interesting in the world of cloud as it is much cheaper than to purchase extra HDD or SSD at higher rates. Google One comes to this point straight and that too with even lower charges for the same amount of storage. From India's point of view, its key audience should be mid-range mobile holders who face the issue of storage with an internal memory of 32GB to 64GB and depends largely on cloud providers like Google or Microsoft for extra reliable storage. The beauty of it doesn't lie in its prices but the range it provides, it provides paid plans starting from 100GB at Rs.130/monthly at lowest to 30TB at Rs.19,500/monthly.I mean 30TB is enough for someone unless he plans to store more than 6million songs or 7500 movies or 9million photos on it. One is now available on Google Play Store where you can directly purchase plans, you can also access it through Web.

16Inch MacBook Pro.

16Inch Macbook Pro is the new member of the premium Macbook series. After its predecessor 15Inch release, Apple faced a lot of criticism about issues related to thermal throttling, butterfly keyboard and other undesirable aspects. So Apple decided to come back strong at the same rate with something everybody is talking about and that is 16Inch deal.
So what is so special right?
Now if you are an avid movie watcher or working photo designer, you seek to have a brilliant display to enjoy your stuff and here comes the bright 16Inch Retina display with P3 wide colour gamut and 500 nits of brightness.
Talking from the core, it has Intel Core i9 processor with 8 cores with 16 threads making it a super deal with not forgetting the desirable 64Gb DDR4 ram.
Now as Apple is not known for great gaming experiences but provides you par average performance with its AMD Radeon 5000 Pro MSeries with 8Gb VRAM creating hype around. Let's address the butterfly in the room, finally a better keyboard with better response time and lesser sound surround time clicking it up.
Better battery life and high security is not a new feature to the series but thunderbolt 3 port worth praises from consumers providing with a flash speed of 40Gbps of transfer rate and supporting up to two 6K display. So, in the end, it is the new beginning for a new improved Macbook series.
